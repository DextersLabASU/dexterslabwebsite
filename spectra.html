INTERACTIVE EXOPLANET SPECTRA VISUALIZER

Welcome to our interactive exoplanet spectra visualization tool! To observe exoplanet atmospheres, we watch them with telescopes and record their “spectra”; the properties of the atmosphere (e.g., gas composition, temperature, etc.) influence what this spectrum will look like. With the help of sliders shown, you can manipulate various properties and change how the spectrum is visualized. Click and drag the sliders to the left or right to adjust the associated properties.
